The Centralized Calendar Project

The project to create a multifaceted, centralized, online community calendar for Muncie began at TheMuncieScene.com in 2003, with a focus on Muncie's arts and entertainment community, as well as community activism. To better incorporate a wider scope of community events and to allow TheMuncieScene.com to return to its focus on local arts and activism, this is being broken off as a separate project, located online at MuncieEvents.com.

The Vision

Muncie's centralized calendar project is supported by the Full Circle Arts Co-op, which believes a strong community should be supported by an all-inclusive promotional tool that serves individuals, small organizations, and large businesses equally, represents all geographical and cultural segments fairly, and is in constant development according to the needs of the community and the advancement of Internet technology.

The Plan

  • Inviting the community to take part in planning the development of the project in monthly meetings
  • Pooling event information into a central database
  • Delivering event information through a variety of methods:
    • Customized calendar at MuncieEvents.com
    • Widgets on community websites showing all events, a specific category of events, or only events taking place at a specific location.
    • A customizable mailing list that delivers daily or weekly emails with upcoming event information.
    • A monthly printed newsletter with upcoming event information.
    • RSS feeds and integration into other online calendar applications.
    • A special version of the online calendar optimized for access with handheld devices.
